To complete the background video effect:

1. Get a Marvel video clip (suggestions):
   - Marvel Studios intro animation
   - Marvel movie trailer clips
   - Marvel character montage
   - Comic book animation

2. Name your video file "marvel-bg.mp4" and place it in this folder

Video Requirements:
- Format: MP4
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher recommended
- Duration: 30 seconds or longer (it will loop)
- File size: Optimize for web (recommend under 10MB)

The video will automatically:
- Play on loop
- Be muted
- Scale to cover the entire background
- Have a dark overlay to ensure form readability
